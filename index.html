About NTLF

Now in its 34th edition, the nasscom Technology & Leadership Forum has, for over three decades, been India's premier platform at the intersection of technology, innovation, and leadership.

The Pillars

1 Innovation X: The Future of Everything
1
Innovation X: The Future of Everything
From self-driving cities to quantum breakthroughs, technology and imagination now converge to reshape life, work, and identity - transforming science fiction into the blueprint of our living future.
2 The Intelligent Leader
2
The Intelligent Leader
Stability is gone, and experimentation rules. Today's leader fuses strategy, technology, and empathy, guiding organizations through volatility with audacity, adaptability, and moral clarity.
3 The Global Reorder
3
The Global Reorder
A multipolar and data-driven world is redrawing power and trade. As digital borders rise, resilience, foresight, and collaboration become the real levers of influence.
4 The Reinvented Enterprise
4
The Reinvented Enterprise
Cognitive systems, cloud, and human ingenuity converge to reinvent business - turning cost into transformation, embedding ethics in code, and building adaptive, purpose-led organizations fit for constant change.
